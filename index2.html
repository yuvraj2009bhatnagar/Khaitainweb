    Conclusion


    Electronic resource strength is additional skills of ICT development and timely-up date information services. where as the electronic resource weakness is that a computer, tablet, phone or any device are required and bandwidth, need of technical skills are also required.
    some Key charecteristics of E-resources are that they can be very comprehensive of everything, do not require physical space, feasible in full content search ability for which less time and less paper is required and hyperlinks lead the user to the required information sources and easy in archiving usefull content.
    Thus they are really usefull for teachers and students as this enhances their learning experience and gives them more freedom to do reaserch of their own interests and makes it easier for the teachers to enhance their knowledge as well of these resources and their students future academic growth.
